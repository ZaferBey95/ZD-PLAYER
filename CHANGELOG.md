# Changelog

## 1.1 - 2026-03-08

### Turkce

- Oynatici kontrolleri yenilendi: onceki/sonraki icerik gecisi eklendi, 10 saniye ileri/geri butonlari yeniden konumlandirildi.
- Normal pencere ve tam ekran icin klavye davranislari iyilestirildi; `Space`, `F`, `F11` ve `M` kisayollari guncellendi.
- Tam ekranda icerik degistirirken pencere moduna donme problemi giderildi.
- M3U hesaplarinda dogrudan medya URI'leri, TLS dogrulamasi ve son izlenen dizi bolumu hatirlama akisi duzeltildi.
- FPS gostergesi gercek zamanli olcumle iyilestirildi; ses denetimi icin acilir slider paneli eklendi.
- Oynatici kontrol cubugu ve ses paneli tema ile uyumlu olacak sekilde yeniden tasarlandi.

### English

- Player controls were refreshed with previous/next content navigation, and the 10-second seek buttons were repositioned.
- Keyboard handling was improved for both windowed and fullscreen playback; `Space`, `F`, `F11`, and `M` shortcuts were updated.
- Fixed the issue where switching content in fullscreen forced the player back to windowed mode.
- Corrected direct media URI playback, TLS verification handling for M3U accounts, and remember-last-series-episode behavior.
- Improved the FPS indicator with real-time measurements and added a popover-based volume slider panel.
- Redesigned the player control bar and volume panel to better match the application's visual theme.
